These are the RAW student census files from the DfE NPD request. They should all be named:
 - KS4: KS4Pupil_YEAR_Census e.g. KS4Pupil_2012_Census
 - KS5: KS5CandInd_YEAR_KS4_KS3_Census e.g. KS5CandInd_2012_KS4_KS3_Census
 
 
 To find out how to source these files, see: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/national-pupil-database-apply-for-a-data-extract
 
 This folder contains confidential data and should NEVER be comitted to GIT
